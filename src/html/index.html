Welcome to Pokémon PvC

Pokémon PvP is a fighting game inspired by the Pokémon franchise, where you can battle against the computer in exciting strategic combat with a modern and intuitive design.

Get Started

Key Features

Battle System

Turn-based combat with updated and balanced mechanics, with over 251 available Pokémon.

Auto-Save

Your progress is automatically saved in an SQLite database so you can continue anytime.

Artificial Intelligence

Smart opponents that adapt to your moves and strategies.

Battle System

Learn how battles work in Pokémon PvP and master the strategies to become a true Pokémon master.

Basic Combat

Battles are turn-based. Each turn you can select one of the following actions:

1

Attack

Select one of your Pokémon's moves to attack the opponent. Each move has different type, power and accuracy.

2

Surrender

Give up the current battle if you think you can't win. Your progress will be saved and you can try again later.

Attack Result

When a Pokémon runs out of HP or stamina, what is shown in the image happens: the Pokémon can no longer continue and the battle ends for it. At this point, you must choose another Pokémon from your team to continue the battle.

Battle Result

When one of the teams runs out of Pokémon able to battle, the results screen appears showing the winner and the rewards earned. Here you can see which team won, which Pokémon fainted, the experience gained, and any items or progress unlocked as a result of the match.
